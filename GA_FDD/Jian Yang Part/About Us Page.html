About Us

Welcome to TenMin Sport, a basketball club founded in 2021. We offer opportunities to train and build lifelong connections both on and off the court. Whether you're a mastered player or a greenhand, TenMin Sports is your home for increase your skills and sharing unforgettable moments with a our team.

Whether you are here to play, learn or help others, TenMin Sports invites you come to join our big familiy. Let's play, share and grow together !
